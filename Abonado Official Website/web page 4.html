How do We Prevent Poverty?

Preventing poverty requires a multifaceted approach.

  • First, promote economic growth through policies that create jobs and improve incomes.
  • Second, invest in education and healthcare to ensure everyone has the skills and health needed to participate in the economy.
  • Third, improve access to basic services like clean water, sanitation, and housing.
  • Fourth, support social protection systems to help those who cannot support themselves.
  • Fifth, empower marginalized communities by involving them in decision-making processes.
  • Finally, address inequality by removing barriers to resources and opportunities.
